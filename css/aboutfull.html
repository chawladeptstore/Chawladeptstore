Our Story
In 1989, Chawla Departmental store first started as a Gaming Parlour and it started with pretty good pace and after continuing it for like year or so we decided to start the work of Cable and renting, in which we used to give VCR's and Colour Television on Rent, as Colour Televions were not very much popular at that point of time. Gaming parlour and Cable were going parallel
After continuing the work of VCR's and Renting we decided to expand and We started with Grocery Store as "Chawla Departmental Store" in which we provide all kind of grocery stuff and after that we keep expanding to Stationary, Milk Dairy and Telecom Services and from then We're providing service to Household's and Office's nearbuy!
